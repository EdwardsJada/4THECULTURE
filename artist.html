ARTIST PROFILE

RIHANNA — Icons. Influence. Impact.

Robyn Rihanna Fenty

Robyn Rihanna Fenty is more than a global superstar — she’s a cultural phenomenon. Born in Barbados, Rihanna first captured the world’s attention with her breakout single “Pon de Replay” in 2005. Since then, she has evolved into one of the most influential artists of her generation, with numerous chart-topping hits across genres, from pop and R&B to dancehall and hip-hop.

But Rihanna’s impact extends far beyond the music industry. She has become a force in fashion and beauty, breaking traditional standards through her inclusive Fenty Beauty line, Savage X Fenty lingerie brand, and her fearless personal style. Her presence on the red carpet is as iconic as her voice on the mic — bold, unapologetic, and trendsetting.

“I don’t do things for the response or the controversy. I just live my life. I want to be remembered as someone who was fearless.” — Rihanna
